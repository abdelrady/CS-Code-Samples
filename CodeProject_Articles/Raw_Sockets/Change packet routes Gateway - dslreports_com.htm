2- How do i choose another Concentrator if i know its IP? (i think that was my original question)
You won't achieve much without you and the destination IP address within the same network (same BGP AS).

said by os008 :

1- How do i skip SeaBone to Telia please?
Have yourself and the destination IP address to be within the same network (same BGP AS). For instance; if the destination IP address is within Level3 network, then you should be connected to also Level3 network (the same BGP AS to be precise) to skip intermediate ISP such as SeaBone and Telia to have fastest connection.

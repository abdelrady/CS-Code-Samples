  • CodeProject.com MVP

Each month the best article in each category will be awarded a swag of prizes. Every month we'll put together a bunch of prizes from different companies and it's winner takes all!

To be eligible articles must be new and must be related to Visual Studio and .NET development in some form or another. Check out the submission guidelines for information on posting articles. You may only enter where local laws and international export laws allow your participation. Judges decision is final, and prizes and values may be changed at any time. Deliveries to some locations may require an import duty or tax to be paid. Winners will be responsible for paying all duties and taxes owing.

To Enter

  • Please read guidlines for submitting an article
  • You can submit either via the Submission Wizard or by sending to us directly

Example
wProfessor Solomon claims that the W(n lg n) lower bound for sorting n numbers does not apply to his computer environment, in which the control flow of a program can split three ways after a single comparison ai : aj, according to whether ai < aj, ai = aj, or ai > aj.  Show that the professor is wrong by proving that the number of three-way comparisons required to sort n element is still W(n lg n).
w
wThe minimum height of a decision tree split three ways is       log3 n!
wlog3 n! = lg n! – lg 3 = c lg n! = Q(n lg n)
wLower bound = W(n lg n)

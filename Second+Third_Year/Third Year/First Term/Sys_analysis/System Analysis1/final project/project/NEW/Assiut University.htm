Assiut University was established in October 1957 as the first university in Upper Egypt to prepare graduates who are equipped with the foundations of specialized scientific knowledge and who are highly trained in the skills required for the field of specialization,while the university programs accord with the changes of the age The university is keen on using its human resources,research capabilities and counseling experiences in solving environmental problems and serving service and production sectors to achieve total development The university seeks to provide the basic elements of the continuous development of education in general and of the university and higher education in particular to keep up with the rapid technological and scientific developments.

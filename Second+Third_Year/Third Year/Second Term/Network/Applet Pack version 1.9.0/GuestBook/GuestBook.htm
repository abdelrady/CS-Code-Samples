GuestBook

 

The GuestBook applet provides a zone where visitors can send you a message by email. Once you have received the mail you decide ot not to put the messages in the file "guestbook.txt". The applet displays the messages in this file. As applets can't connect to remote hosts, this applet connects to your local server. So your web server must also be a mail server. See "GuestBook1.0.txt" for further details.

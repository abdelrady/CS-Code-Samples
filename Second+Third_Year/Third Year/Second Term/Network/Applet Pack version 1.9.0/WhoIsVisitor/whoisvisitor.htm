WhoIsVisitor

 

The WhoIsVisitor applet sends you a mail with some informations about the visitors of your site: the OS name used, and also the last visited URL which can help you to know how people come on your site. As applets can't connect to remote hosts, this applet connects to your local server. So your web server must also be a mail server. See "WhoIsVisitor1.0.txt" for further details. The applet size is 3x3 or something like that because the grey applet zone is no use for this applet.
